About Libraria 📚

Our Online Library is a digital space created to make discovering and borrowing books easier and more organized. We built this platform to simplify the traditional library experience and bring it into a smarter, more accessible environment.

What You Can Do Here

  • Search for books by title, author, or category.
  • Explore available books and view detailed information.
  • Check whether a book is available or currently borrowed.
  • Borrow books with a simple and clear process.
  • Keep track of the books you have borrowed.
  • Save your favourite books for quick access.

Why We Built It

As students, we understand how important it is to access knowledge quickly and efficiently. We wanted to create a system that keeps books organized, easy to manage, and simple to explore.

This platform represents our vision of how technology can improve the way libraries work — making them faster, clearer, and more user-friendly.

Because knowledge grows when it is shared.
